About the Occasion

A tribute to the poet of song, soul, and sunrise

Rabindra Jayanti marks the birth anniversary of Rabindranath Tagore, whose writing, music, paintings, and vision continue to shape Bengal's cultural imagination and India's artistic spirit.

This celebration by Class 8-E invites the audience into a calm, richly textured atmosphere where Rabindra Sangeet, recitation, and performance come together like pages from an illuminated manuscript.
